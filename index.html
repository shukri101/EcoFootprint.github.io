How are the calculations made?

The carbon footprint of a borehole drilling project is calculated using the following formula:

total emissions = fuel consumption * emissions factor + materials used * emissions factor + waste generated * emissions factor

where the emissions factor is a value that represents the amount of carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions per unit of fuel or materials. We use a default value of 2.3 kilograms of CO2 per liter of fuel and 0.43 kilograms of CO2 per kilogram of materials.
